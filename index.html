What is Shuhari?

An Explosive Day Full of Expression and Dialogue

SHUHARI is a borderless festival where expressions, research, and inquiries are brought together, sparking unexpected encounters and possibilities for new creations.

Event Schedule

Explore our full day program featuring workshops, performances, and interactive sessions from 10:00 to 19:00, followed by a closing party. The schedule includes traditional Japanese tea ceremonies, dance performances, storytelling, and group discussions.

Learn, challenge, and find your own path. We welcome visitors to experience this borderless festival of expression and dialogue.
