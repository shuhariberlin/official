What is Shuhari?

An Explosive Day Full of Expression and Dialogue

Shuhari is a borderless festival where expressions, research, and inquiries are brought together, sparking unexpected encounters and possibilities for new creations.

The concept of "Shu-Ha-Ri" represents the journey of learning and mastery: "Shu" (obey) - learn the fundamentals, "Ha" (break) - detach from traditional forms, and "Ri" (separate) - transcend to create your own path.

Join us for a day where creators, researchers, and enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds come together to share ideas, challenge conventions, and find their unique creative voice.
